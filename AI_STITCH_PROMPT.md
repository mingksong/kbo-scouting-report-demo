## Interaction Specifications

### Gestures
- **Swipe Left/Right**: Switch between tabs
- **Pull Down**: Refresh data
- **Tap Card Header**: Expand/collapse category
- **Long Press Player Card**: Quick actions (favorite, compare)

### Animations
- **Page Transition**: Slide from right
- **Tab Switch**: Fade + slight slide
- **Radar Chart**: Draw animation on load
- **Progress Bars**: Fill animation (0.3s ease-out)

### Loading States
- Skeleton screens for cards
- Pulsing radar chart placeholder
- Shimmer effect on text

- **Small Phone** (< 375px): Single column, smaller radar

- **Large Phone** (> 414px): Larger radar, more spacing
